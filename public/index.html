Transcription runs on the local service, not in this tab — the audio is split into segments and processed across several worker threads in parallel, so your browser stays responsive. Bigger models are more accurate but slower; pick the language model for non-English audio and optionally type the language.

Burn into video is done by ffmpeg on the backend. Soft adds a fast, lossless, toggleable subtitle track (recommended). Hard permanently draws the captions onto the picture (needs an ffmpeg built with libass). Output is an .mp4. Or export an .srt to keep captions separate and editable.
